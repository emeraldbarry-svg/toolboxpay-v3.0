Operating Manual

1. Navigation: Use the top bar to jump between tools. The active tool will glow Orange & White.

2. Smart Quotes: Select a client, add labour or material lines, and hit 'Send'. It formats everything for WhatsApp instantly.

3. Job Gallery: Take photos and Tag them to a client. Use the Filter at the top to see only that job's history.

4. Profile: Enter your bank details once in the 'Profile' section; they are saved automatically for every quote you send.
