TOOLBOXGUIDE

QUICK START
01
Configuration

Go to Business Profile. Enter your trading name and bank details. This ensures every settlement you send is professional and ready for payment.

02
Database

Use the Client Database to save names and addresses. When you start a job, just select them from the list to auto-fill the header.

03
Calculation

Enter your labour costs and add materials one by one. Toggle VAT if you are registered to ensure the total is accurate.

04
Finalise

Hit SAVE & SHARE. This logs the job in your History and opens the print menu to send a PDF or message to your client.

Privacy Note

All data is stored in your browser's local memory. If you clear your browser cache, you will lose your data. Use the Backup feature in Settings regularly.
